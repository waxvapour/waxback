mirror imaging

what ?

bit by bit copy of computer hard drive.


why ?

there is a high chances that during forigncics investigation process the digital evidence can be altered which will make it really hard to get evidence in a presentable state or even destroy it to the extend that its intigrity is lost, so for the sake of Risk Mitigation mirror imaging is done to perform the investigation over it leaveing the actual system unchanged.


How ?

there is a variety of tools for this process to take place includeing HARDWARE & SOFTWARE based tools.
